My name is Rishi Nitin Agrawal.

I began playing chess in 9th grade, when a chess craze swept across high school. I never quite understood how to solve a Rubiks's Cube and didn't care for Beyblades, so I sat those two waves out. But I fell in love with chess, and I've been playing it (off and on) ever since. I was even Head Arbiter for a school tournament we organized!

I might have been inspired by one of my best friends. We used to visit each others' houses to just play chess. I still remember the one time I let him rampage across my side of the board while I set up a sneaky attack and won.

Never quite good but forever enamoured by the beauty of chess, I created this website in July 2025 as a quiet place to reflect on this game.

I have endeavored to make this website a small labor of love. I have written it entirely by hand, populated it with quotes that have personally inspired me, and built it with nothing but raw HTML, CSS, and JavaScript without any frameworks without any frameworks whatsoever.

This website was directly and heavily inspired by musicthoughts.com

Like it, this website will remain a totally free, open, non-commercial website, here only as inspiration and to ponder the beauty of chess
